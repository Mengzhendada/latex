    \\
    $\mathcal{R} = \mathcal{R}_x *\mathcal{R}_z$
    \\
   $ \mathcal{R} = 
    \begin{bmatrix}
      0 & -1 & 0 \\
      cos(\theta) & 0 & -sin(\theta) \\
      sin(\theta) & 0 & cos(\theta)
    \end{bmatrix}
 $
    \\
    $\vec{p}_{spectrometer} = \mathcal{R}*\vec{p}_{beamline}$
